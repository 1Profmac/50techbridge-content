## THE AI AFTER 50 + AI IN THE WORKFORCE ANGLES

### Why AI changes everything for adults 50+

- AI levels the playing field. A 62-year-old with 30 years of industry expertise + AI tools can outcompete a 28-year-old with 3 years of experience
- AI eliminates the "I can't afford a team" barrier — the agentic workforce IS the team
- AI tools don't require coding knowledge — they require judgment, context, and experience. Adults 50+ have all three
- The skills AI cannot replace: relationship-building, institutional memory, ethical judgment, emotional intelligence, pattern recognition from decades of experience — all 50+ strengths
- AI is not taking experienced workers' jobs. It's amplifying them.

### AI in the 50+ Workforce — the employer angle (LinkedIn B2B)
- Employers with 50+ workforces can use AI training to extend the productive years of their most experienced employees
- The cost of losing an experienced worker: institutional knowledge, training costs, transition costs
- AI reskilling is cheaper than replacement — and produces better outcomes
- 50+ workers trained in AI tools have highest adoption rates when training is designed for them (peer model, 3X industry completion rate)
