### Week 1-2: The Workforce Consult

Before anything else, we need 30 minutes.

Not a sales call. A diagnostic.

We need to understand:

- **Your population.** How many employees or participants are 50+? What roles? What's their current tech comfort level?
- **Your funding.** Are you using WIOA ITAs? Organizational training budgets? Grant funds? Each has different requirements.
- **Your reporting needs.** What outcomes do you need to show? Completion rates? Skills assessments? Participant surveys? We build our reporting around your requirements.
- **Your constraints.** Is training during work hours or after? On-site or virtual? Do participants have their own devices?

This conversation takes 30 minutes and tells both of us whether 50+TechBridge is the right fit.

If it's not, we'll tell you. We'd rather save you time than sell you something that won't work for your specific situation.

### Week 2-3: Cohort Design

Once we've confirmed the fit, we design your pilot cohort.

**Cohort size:** 20-50 participants. Small enough to track individually. Large enough to generate meaningful data.

**Participant selection:** We help you identify the right first cohort. Typically, it's workers who are engaged and motivated but struggling with technology adoption. Not the early adopters (they don't need us) and not the actively resistant (they need a different conversation first).

**Schedule:** The 50+TechBridge program is self-paced with structured milestones. Participants can work through modules during designated training time or on their own schedule. Most complete within 6 weeks.

**Access:** Each participant gets a login, access to all 6 modules of the AI curriculum, the peer community, and AI-powered agent instructors available 24/7.

**Cost:** $98 per participant. No contracts. No minimum commitments. No platform fees.

### Week 3-4: Launch

Launch day is simple.

Participants receive their access. They start Module 1. The peer community activates.

Here's what they experience:

**Module 1:** What is AI and why does it matter for me? (Not the 25-year-old version. The version for someone who's been working for 30 years and wonders if any of this is relevant.)

**Module 2:** AI tools I can use today. Hands-on, practical, immediately applicable.

**Module 3:** Building my first AI workflow. A real project, not a simulation.

**Module 4:** AI for my specific role. Customized applications based on what they actually do at work.

**Module 5:** Digital presence and professional identity. Updating their LinkedIn, their professional brand, their visibility.

**Module 6:** What's next. Continuing education, community engagement, advanced applications.

Each module is designed for adults 50+. The pace is right. The language is right. The examples are relevant.

Agent instructors — AI-powered tutors — are available 24/7 for questions. No stupid questions. Infinite patience. Personalized guidance.

### Week 4-8: Progress and Support

During the program, you'll have visibility into:

- **Completion rates by module** — who's progressing, who's stuck
- **Engagement metrics** — community participation, question frequency
- **Confidence assessments** — pre and post surveys measuring technology comfort

If participants get stuck, the peer community and agent instructors intervene before they drop off. That's how we maintain 3X the industry completion rate.

We don't wait for your quarterly report to find out someone fell behind. We catch it in real time.

### Week 8-10: Results and Reporting

At the end of the pilot, you receive:

**Completion report:** How many participants finished. Which modules had the highest engagement. Where people struggled and how we addressed it.

**Confidence survey results:** Pre-program vs. post-program technology confidence scores. 74% of our Pioneers report increased confidence.

**Participant testimonials:** Real quotes from your employees about their experience. These are gold for your board reports, your grant renewals, and your internal communications.

**Recommendations:** Based on your pilot results, we'll tell you exactly how to scale — how many cohorts, what cadence, and what to expect.
