# Part 5 — The Movement
**Theme:** Look how many of us there are.
**Hook:** This isn't one person fighting. This is an army.

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Open wide. Biggest energy of the series. This is the rally.]**

"Five parts."

"Five times we've said the same thing —"

**[Pause. Let the weight build.]**

"You. Are. Not. Done."

**[Beat. Then faster — building momentum.]**

"And you're not saying it alone anymore."

"There are people in this community who were written off at 52."

"At 61. At 67. At 74."

**[Pause. Harder.]**

"Who were handed a gold watch and shown the door."

"Who were passed over for the 28-year-old who didn't know half as much."

"Who were told — politely, professionally —"

**[Cold.]**

"that their time was up."

**[Beat. Then the turn — full force.]**

"Every single one of them showed up anyway."

"Learned the tools. Built the skills. Found the community."

**[Rising.]**

"And now they're not asking for a seat at the table."

**[Full stop.]**

"They're **building their own.**"

**[Pause. Look directly into camera. This is the close.]**

"This is 50+TechBridge."

"This is what a movement looks like."

"We are not slowing down."

"We are not apologizing."

"And we are not — **done.**"

**[One beat of silence.]**

"Join us."

**[Hold 2 full seconds. Don't blink. Don't smile. Just certain.]**
