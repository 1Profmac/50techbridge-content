# Part 3 — Real People, Real Results
**Theme:** Testimonial-style. Faces. Names. Proof.
**Hook:** Stop talking about the problem — show the solution working.

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Warmer than Parts 1 & 2 — but still steel underneath.]**

"You've heard the stats."

"Now meet the people."

**[Pause. Direct.]**

"People who were told — by the world, by the industry, by the algorithm —"

"that their time was up."

**[Beat.]**

"They disagreed."

**[Names — say them like they matter. Because they do.]**

"A 67-year-old who learned to build websites."

"A 71-year-old running her own online business."

"A 58-year-old who said — I'm not retiring. I'm **re-launching.**"

**[Pause. Let that land.]**

"These aren't exceptions."

"These are **members** of 50+TechBridge."

**[Lean in — back to the fight.]**

"Free training. Real support. Zero condescension."

"We don't water it down because of your age."

"We meet you where you are — and we take you **further.**"

**[Final line. Eyes forward. Certain.]**

"This is what fighting back looks like."

"Join us."

**[Hold.]**
