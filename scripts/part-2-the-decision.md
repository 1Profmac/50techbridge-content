# Part 2 — The Decision
**Theme:** They had the data. They chose anyway.
**Hook:** This wasn't ignorance — it was a deliberate decision.

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Beat. Lean in slightly.]**

"Part two. Because we're not done."

**[Pause.]**

"Here's what they don't want you to know."

"The ad industry **has** the data."

"They **know** adults 50 and over control $76 trillion."

**[Slower. Deliberate.]**

"They know you buy cars. You book travel. You remodel homes. You invest."

**[Pause. Harder.]**

"And they **still** chose to spend their money on someone else."

**[Beat. Cold.]**

"That's not ignorance."

"That's a **decision.**"

**[Shift — heat rising.]**

"They decided your money was welcome."

"**You** were not."

**[Final turn — battle cry.]**

"50+TechBridge says different."

"We see you. We're training you. We're building with you."

**[Dead eyes to camera. Final line.]**

"Their loss."

"**Your** move."

**[Hold. Don't look away.]**
