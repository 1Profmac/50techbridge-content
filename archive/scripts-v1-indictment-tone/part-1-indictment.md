# Part 1 — The Indictment
**Theme:** 5% of ad spend vs $76 trillion in wealth
**Hook:** The gap that isn't a gap — it's ageism

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Beat. Look dead into camera. Don't blink.]**

"The ad industry spends **five percent** of its dollars on adults 50 and over."

**[Pause. Let it land.]**

"Adults 50 and over control **seventy-six trillion dollars** in wealth."

**[Slow head shake. Controlled anger.]**

"That is not a gap."

**[Harder.]**

"That is **ageism.** And it is **economic discrimination.**"

**[Forward lean. Direct.]**

"You built this economy. You raised families, ran companies, paid taxes for **decades** —"

**[Beat.]**

"— and they act like you don't exist."

**[Pause. Shift tone — now a battle cry.]**

"**50+TechBridge** is fighting back."

"Free AgeTech training. Real community. Proven results."

**[Final line — slow, certain, eyes forward.]**

"You are **not** done."

"And **neither are we.**"

**[Hold 1 second. Don't look away.]**

---

## Delivery Notes

| Moment | Direction |
|---|---|
| "five percent" | Say it slowly — almost disgusted |
| "seventy-six trillion" | Emphasis on every syllable |
| "That is ageism" | Flat, declarative — no yelling, just cold truth |
| "you don't exist" | Slight pause before — let the insult breathe |
| "fighting back" | First moment of heat — energy shift |
| "You are not done" | Slow. Certain. Eye contact. |
