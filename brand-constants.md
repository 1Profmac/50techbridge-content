## 12. DATA SOVEREIGNTY POLICY

**Rule: We publish on their platforms. We own the relationship. Always.**

LMT is a global community. No single nation's data laws govern us beyond
what protects our Pioneers. We meet or exceed GDPR — the strongest global standard.

| Layer | What We Own | Where It Lives |
|---|---|---|
| Pioneer profiles | Name, email, progress, completion, badges, referrals | LearnDash + BuddyBoss (self-hosted) |
| Community activity | Posts, connections, engagement, groups | BuddyBoss (self-hosted) |
| Email list | Every Pioneer, partner, prospect — segmented | ConvertKit — weekly CSV backup |
| Course data | Progress, completion, quiz results | LearnDash (self-hosted) |
| Social analytics | Reach, engagement, clicks | Metricool — CSV export weekly |
| Website analytics | All visitor data | Plausible (privacy-first, no Google) |
| Video masters | All production files | Bunny.net CDN — not YouTube only |
| Government pipeline | All contract and grant data | Airtable — full CSV export |

**We never use:**
- Facebook Groups as primary community (platform can delete)
- Google Analytics (feeds Google our user data)
- Third-party course platforms (we own LearnDash)
- YouTube as sole video storage (masters on Bunny.net)

**Social platforms = distribution channels only. Our ecosystem = the relationship.**
