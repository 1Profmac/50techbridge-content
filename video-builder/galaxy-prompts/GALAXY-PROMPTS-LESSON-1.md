## HOW TO CREATE IN GALAXY

1. Open Galaxy AI (galaxy.ai or your Galaxy app)
2. Select **Video Generation** or **AI Video**
3. Paste the **Scene Description** into the scene/visual prompt
4. Paste the **Script** into the dialogue/narration field
5. Set duration: **8-10 seconds**
6. Set aspect ratio: **16:9 landscape**
7. Generate → review → download MP4
8. Save to this folder with the exact filename listed
9. Repeat for all 8 clips

## RULES FOR ALL CLIPS

- Adults must look 60+ (grey hair, mature faces)
- Diverse: Black, Latino, Asian, White — men and women
- Real domestic settings: kitchen, living room, doctor office, community center
- Warm lighting in every scene
- Devices are natural parts of life — not foreign objects
- People look confident and in control — never confused or helpless
- The device feels like a friend, not a challenge
- Never use: "senior", "elderly", condescending expressions, cold lighting
