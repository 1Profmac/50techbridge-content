# HOW TO CREATE IN GALAXY AI

1. Open Galaxy AI on your Samsung device
2. Select **Video Generation** or **AI Video**
3. Paste the **Scene Description** into the scene/visual prompt
4. Paste the **Script** into the dialogue/narration field
5. Set duration: **~1.5 minutes** (90 seconds)
6. Set aspect ratio: **16:9 landscape**
7. Set quality: **1080p**
8. Generate → review → download MP4
9. Save to the correct `layers/clips/lesson-X/` folder with the exact filename listed
10. Repeat for all clips

## RULES FOR ALL CLIPS

- Adults must look 60+ (grey hair, mature faces, reading glasses OK)
- Diverse: Black, Latino, Asian, White — men and women equally
- Real domestic settings: kitchen, living room, dining room, home office, community center
- Warm golden lighting in every scene — never cold, harsh, or clinical
- Devices are natural parts of life — not foreign objects
- People look confident and in control — never confused, frustrated, or helpless
- Technology feels like a tool they own, not something that owns them
- Never use: "senior", "elderly", condescending expressions, cold sterile lighting
