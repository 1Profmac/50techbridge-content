# Skill: YouTube Short — Opportunity Tone
**File:** skills/yt-short-opportunity.md
**Copy to:** ~/.claude/commands/yt-short-opportunity.md to activate as /yt-short-opportunity

---

Generate a complete opportunity-framed YouTube Short content package for 50+TechBridge.

## Usage
`/yt-short-opportunity [topic] [part-number]`

Examples:
- `/yt-short-opportunity "the spending data" 6`
- `/yt-short-opportunity "tech skills for 50+" 7`
- `/yt-short-opportunity "corporate ageism cost" 8`

---

## Tone Rules

**This is NOT an indictment. It is an illumination.**

- Frame every problem as a missed opportunity
- Give organizations the benefit of the doubt — momentum, not malice
- Make the reader/viewer feel urgency from FOMO, not guilt
- The 50+ community is the asset, not the victim
- Data does the heavy lifting — let numbers be the argument
- Close every script with an invitation, not a demand

**The reader finishes thinking:** "We need to move on this."
**Not thinking:** "We're bad people."

---

## What to Generate

### 1. HeyGen Script (~28 seconds)
- Delivery stage directions in `[brackets]`
- Calm, confident, collegial opening
- Data reveal in the middle
- Forward-looking, opportunity close
- Final line: an invitation or a question that lingers

### 2. Delivery Notes Table
`| Moment | Direction |`

### 3. On-Screen Captions
`| # | Time | Text | Notes |`
- 3 power frame moments (full screen)
- Key stats in gold
- Questions in white italic

### 4. Ticker Bar Copy
- Gold bar: short punchy phrases, ALL CAPS, ◆ separators
- Dark bar: longer data phrases, ▸ separators

### 5. YouTube Metadata
- 3 title options ranked
- Full description
- 20 tags

### 6. Cross-Platform Posts
- LinkedIn (peer-to-peer, data-forward)
- Instagram caption + hashtags
- Instagram Story sequence (5 frames)
- Pinned YouTube comment
