# Part 5 — The Invitation
**Tone:** Opportunity / Call to action — warm but certain
**Hook:** The window is open. What you do next is the choice.

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Open warm. This is the close of the series. Bring both audiences home.]**

"Five parts."

"One number: $76 trillion."

"One gap: 5% of ad spend."

"One question: what are you going to do about it?"

**[Pause. Softer now — this is the invitation.]**

"If you're an organization —"

"the opportunity to own this market is right now."

"Before your competition figures it out."

"Before the window closes."

**[Shift — now speaking to the 50+ viewer.]**

"And if you're 50 plus —"

"the tools, the training, and the community"

"that the world forgot to build for you?"

**[Warm.]**

"We built it."

**[Beat. Both audiences now.]**

"50+TechBridge."

"Free AgeTech training. Real community. Proven results."

"347 Pioneers. 23 partner organizations. Growing every day."

**[Final line. Certain. Generous. Forward.]**

"The overlooked trillion is waiting."

"Come be part of what happens next."

**[Hold 2 seconds.]**
