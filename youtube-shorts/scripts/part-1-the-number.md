# Part 1 — The Number Nobody Is Talking About
**Tone:** Opportunity / Revelation
**Hook:** The most important number in marketing right now

---

## HeyGen Script

**[Calm. Confident. Like someone about to show you something you can't unsee.]**

"I want to show you a number."

**[Pause.]**

"$76 trillion."

**[Beat.]**

"That is the combined wealth of adults 50 and older — worldwide."

"More than any other demographic on the planet."

**[Pause. Let it register.]**

"Now here's the part that surprises everyone."

"The advertising industry spends just **5%** of its budget reaching them."

**[Slight shake of the head. Not angry — almost amazed.]**

"Five percent. For the wealthiest consumer group in human history."

**[Direct. Clear.]**

"That's not a strategy. That's a blind spot."

"And blind spots — in business — are opportunities."

**[Warm but certain.]**

"50+TechBridge exists to close that gap."

"For the organizations missing it. And for the people being missed."

**[Final line. Eyes forward.]**

"The number is $76 trillion."

"Now you can't say you didn't know."

**[Hold.]**

---

## Delivery Notes

| Moment | Direction |
|---|---|
| "$76 trillion" | Say it slowly — let the size land |
| "More than any other demographic" | Slight emphasis — this is the reveal |
| "Five percent" | Measured disbelief — not outrage, just... really? |
| "That's a blind spot" | Matter-of-fact, not accusatory |
| "Blind spots are opportunities" | This is the turn — energy lifts |
| "Now you can't say you didn't know" | Warm smile. Invitation, not threat. |
