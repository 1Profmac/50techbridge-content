## The Bridge Visual (center)

**What to search in Canva:** `suspension bridge wireframe gold blue`

**Or use two images:**
- Left bridge: search `golden gate bridge orange warm` — set blend to **Screen**, opacity 60%
- Right bridge: search `bridge circuit blueprint blue` — set blend to **Screen**, opacity 60%

**They should meet in the center** with a small gold glow dot at the connection point.

**Global connection lines:** Search `world network connections map` — overlay at 15% opacity in gold tones.

## Why Claude as Co-Author

This is not a gimmick. It is the thesis of the book — proven.

Brian McKinney used AI as a co-creator, co-researcher, and
co-builder to construct a global movement. The book documents that
journey. Having Claude on the cover is the most honest and pioneering
thing the book can do.

It makes Brian the first author in the 50+ space — and one of the
first anywhere — to build transparently with AI and put it on the cover.

**That is a TED Talk.** That is a keynote. That is a movement.
